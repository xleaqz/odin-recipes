Chicken Wings

Description

Chicken Wings are very popular and delicious

Ingredients

  • Chicken wings.
  • Seafood seasoning.
  • Cornstach.
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Place the wings in a large bowl. Add seafood seasoning and massage it into the wings.Add cornstach and toss to coat.Let the wings rest for 10 minutes.
  2. Preheat an grill to 500 degrees and lighlty oil the grate.
  3. Place the wings onto the grate and wait 10 minutes. After 10 minutes flip the wings and cook it another 10 minutes.
  4. Heat butter and season it. finally serve it.
