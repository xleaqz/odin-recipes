pancake

Desciption

pancake is a delicious cake for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Flour.
  • Eggs.
  • Sugar.
  • Milk.
  • Vanillin sugar.
  • Baking powder.

Steps

  1. Combine flour with milk and dry it in a large bowl.
  2. Add eggs, sugar and vanillin sugar and mix it togheter.
  3. Cook it in a pan until its getting a little brown.
  4. Add some toppings (optional)
Enjoy it :)
